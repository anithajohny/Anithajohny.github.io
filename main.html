Electronic Documents


E-DocS also known as Electronic Documents is a digital platform to download and read different types of books, poems, journals etc for free. This is an opportunity for learning students and working professionals in which researchers can find journals and also publish their own work with credits. As this is an open website, anyone can access any data and keep track of their work. Different functionalities which we provide in this website are:


  1. Read books online and offline
  2. Download books and journals
  3. Upload your own articles including journals
